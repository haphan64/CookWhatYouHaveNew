    Cook What You Have!!!

    Quarantine Special!!

    Have you ever stared into the abyss of your fridge wondering what you can make with just apples, flour, and milk? Look no further! Cook What You Have will take a list of ingredients you have on hand and suggest recipes you can make using those ingredients. You may have to venture out to obtain more ingredients, however, with beautiful images of all the ingredients in the recipe, you will have no trouble finding what you need to make the perfect meal!
